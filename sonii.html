Sonii

Sonii is an iPhone audio utility app built for direct listening workflows. The product focus is speed, simplicity, and keeping audio interaction lightweight instead of bloated.

What It Solves

Some audio apps try to do too much at once. Sonii is positioned around faster access to the listening task itself, with less navigation overhead and less UI drag between intention and playback.

Who It Is For

  • Users who want simpler audio interaction on iPhone.
  • People who prefer focused workflows over feature-heavy interfaces.
  • Anyone looking for a lightweight utility instead of an all-in-one media suite.
