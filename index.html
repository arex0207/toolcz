Before you send a message

If your question is about an App Store listing, policy review, or ad verification, the document links above are the fastest source of record. For product support, include enough context so the issue can be reproduced quickly.

  • Include the app name, iPhone model, and iOS version.
  • Add the app version and a short description of what happened.
  • Mention whether the issue is about support, policy, billing, or store review.
Public support pages, privacy documents, terms, and app-ads.txt are all published under the same domain for consistency.
